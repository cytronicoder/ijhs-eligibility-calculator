Important Note on IJHS Points & Assessment Year

IJHS Scholarship Points (SPs) do not accumulate across years.
Students must earn the required points within a single Assessment Year to be eligible for induction.

An Assessment Year runs from August 1st to July 31st the following year.
E.g., Assessment Year 2024-25 is August 1, 2024 through July 31, 2025.
Only contests held within this timeframe will count toward that year's SP total.

PAExS Awards (STEAM Global Recognition)

  • PAExS: Gold in any 2 of IJMO, VIJSO, IJIO, IJCO, IJAO → +2 SPs
  • PAExS STAR: Gold in any 3 of above → +3 SPs

SIT Awards (STEAM International Tournaments)

  • SIT Star (total SP ≥ 10) → +3 SPs
  • SIT Platinum (8 - 9.99) → +2 SPs
  • SIT Tri (6 - 7.99) → +1 SP
  • SIT Award (5 - 5.99) → +0.5 SP
